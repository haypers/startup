What is the Public Pixel?

The Public Pixel is a 50 x 50 pixel art canvas that anyone can edit!

Every 30 seconds a user can change the color of a pixle to one of the 5 colors of the day!

Alternatively, select up to 30 pixles to create a 'plan' or a template that other users can see and choose to either help build, or sabotage!
